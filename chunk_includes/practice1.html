Instructions (1/3)

How to Read

Welcome! In this experiment, you'll be reading English sentences and determining whether or not each sentence sounds like acceptable English - natural and 'grammatical.'

The sentences you read will be presented to you automatically, word by word, at a steady pace. It'll seem a bit fast at first, but you'll get used to it quickly. It's about as fast as people normally read words.

Before each sentence, you will see a cross ("+") to indicate that you may begin a sentence. When you see the cross, get ready to read a sentence. It will disappear in one second, and the sentence will begin automatically when it disappears.

Confirm your understanding
